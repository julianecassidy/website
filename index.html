About

My name is Juliane Cassidy. I am currently studying software engineering. Please find my resume here.

Before launching my software engineering career, I worked as a web designer and managed databases for arts non-profits. I self-taught myself code during this work as a way to automate my work and find more efficient solutions for data tracking. However, I quickly found that I enjoyed the coding more than my actual work and decided to make it my career.

Outside of coding, I like to spend time outdoors hiking, camping, and climbing - often with my dog along for the adventure. I am also a historical reenactor for the 1840s and 1890s.
